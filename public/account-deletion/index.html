Account & Data Deletion

HomeFitly respects your right to delete your account and all associated data. This page explains how to permanently remove your account and what happens when you do.

How to Delete Your Account (In-App)

The quickest way to delete your account is directly from the app:

  1. Open the HomeFitly app
  2. Go to the Settings tab
  3. Scroll to the Account section
  4. Tap "Delete Account"
  5. Confirm the deletion when prompted
Important: This permanently deletes your account and all associated data, including tasks, warranties, receipts, contacts, projects, household information, and home profile data.

What Gets Deleted

  • Your account and login credentials
  • Home profile information (address, home details, features)
  • All tasks and maintenance records
  • Warranty records and uploaded documents
  • Receipt data and scanned images
  • Service contact information
  • Home project details, quotes, and photos
  • Household and family member information
  • Notification preferences and app settings

What Happens to Your Subscription

If you have an active Premium subscription, please be aware:

Deleting your account does NOT automatically cancel your Apple subscription.

You must separately cancel your subscription through Apple: open the Settings app > tap your Apple ID > Subscriptions.

You can also manage it through the App Store app.

Can't Access the App?

If you are unable to delete your account through the app, you can request deletion by email:

  • Email support@homefitly.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request"
  • Include the email address associated with your HomeFitly account
  • We will process your request within 30 days
  • You will receive confirmation when your account has been deleted

Data Retention After Deletion

  • Account data is deleted from our servers
  • Some data may persist in encrypted backups for up to 90 days before automatic purging
  • We may retain anonymized, aggregated data that cannot be linked back to you
