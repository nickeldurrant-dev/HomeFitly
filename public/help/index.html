Getting Started

New to HomeFitly? Here's how to get up and running:

  • Create an account using your email address or sign in with Apple.
  • Set up your home profile by entering your address, home type, size, and key features (e.g., pool, garden, HVAC system).
  • Get personalized tasks — HomeFitly automatically generates a maintenance schedule tailored to your home and lifestyle.
  • Browse the Task Catalog to add additional tasks that matter to you.
Account & Login
  • Signing in: Use the email and password you registered with, or use Sign in with Apple if that's how you created your account.
  • Forgot your password? Tap "Forgot Password" on the login screen. We'll send a reset link to your email.
  • Managing your account: Go to Settings within the app to update your name, email, or other account details.
Home Profile

Your home profile helps HomeFitly deliver the right maintenance tasks at the right time.

  • Address: Add or update your home address during onboarding or in Settings.
  • Home details: Specify your home type (house, apartment, condo, etc.), square footage, year built, and number of bedrooms/bathrooms.
  • Features: Tell us about features like a pool, garden, fireplace, or specific HVAC systems so we can recommend relevant tasks.
  • Household info: Add household members to enable family task management.
Tasks & Maintenance
  • Creating tasks: Tap the "+" button on the Tasks screen or browse the Task Catalog to add pre-built maintenance tasks.
  • Reminders: Each task can have a due date and notification reminder so you never miss a maintenance window.
  • Recurring schedules: Set tasks to repeat daily, weekly, monthly, quarterly, or annually.
  • Completing tasks: Mark a task as complete and optionally add notes, photos, or cost information for your records.
  • Task history: View your completion history to track your home maintenance over time.
Projects, Quotes & Contractors
  • Managing projects: Create home improvement projects to organize larger jobs like kitchen remodels, roof repairs, or landscaping.
  • Adding quotes: Attach contractor quotes to any project so you can keep everything in one place.
  • Comparing bids: Use the quote comparison view to evaluate contractors side-by-side on price, timeline, and scope.
  • Tracking progress: Move projects through stages (idea, planning, in-progress, completed) and add photos to document your progress.
Receipts & Documents
  • Scanning receipts: Use the built-in scanner to photograph and store receipts for home purchases and repairs.
  • Warranty documents: Attach warranty paperwork to keep all your coverage details organized.
  • Home records: Store important home documents like inspection reports, insurance documents, and manuals in one convenient location.
Family Management
  • Setting up a household: Create a household and invite family members by sharing your unique household code.
  • Assigning tasks: Assign tasks to specific family members so everyone knows their responsibilities.
  • Task rotation: Set up automatic task rotation so chores are shared fairly among household members.
  • Kid task approval (Premium): Enable approval mode for younger family members — their completed tasks come to you for review before being marked done.
Warranties
  • Adding warranties: Store warranty details for appliances, systems, and home components including purchase date, coverage period, and provider information.
  • Expiration tracking: HomeFitly tracks all your warranty expiration dates in one place so nothing slips through the cracks.
  • Warranty alerts: Receive notifications before a warranty expires so you can take action — whether that means filing a claim or renewing coverage.

Restore Purchases

If you've previously purchased a Premium subscription and need to restore it on a new device or after reinstalling the app:

  • Open HomeFitly and sign in with your account.
  • Go to Settings > Subscription > Restore Purchases.
  • Your Premium access will be restored automatically if an active subscription is found linked to your Apple ID.

If the restore doesn't work, make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you originally used to subscribe.

Manage or Cancel Subscription

Your HomeFitly Premium subscription is managed through Apple. You can manage or cancel it in two ways:

  • From your iPhone/iPad: Open the Settings app > tap your Apple ID (your name at the top) > Subscriptions > select HomeFitly to manage or cancel.
  • From the HomeFitly app: Go to Settings > Subscription > Manage Subscription. This will take you directly to your Apple subscription settings.

If you cancel, your Premium features will remain active until the end of your current billing period.

Reporting a Problem

Found a bug or experiencing an issue? We'd love to help.

  • Send an email to support@homefitly.com describing the problem.
  • Please include your device model, iOS version, and HomeFitly app version (found in Settings) to help us investigate faster.
  • Screenshots or screen recordings are always helpful if the issue is visual.

We take every report seriously and will work to resolve the issue as quickly as possible.

Account & Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time.

  • From the app: Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account. Follow the confirmation prompts to permanently delete your account.
  • This action removes all your data including your home profile, tasks, projects, receipts, and household information.
  • Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

For more information, visit our Account Deletion page.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions.

Is HomeFitly free to use?
Yes! HomeFitly offers a free plan that includes core task management, a home profile, basic reminders, and up to 5 active tasks. You can upgrade to Premium at any time to unlock unlimited tasks, family management, receipt scanning, warranty tracking, and more.
What's included in Premium?
Premium gives you unlimited tasks and projects, family household management with task assignment and rotation, kid task approval, receipt and document scanning, warranty tracking with expiration alerts, contractor quote comparison, and advanced task recommendations. It's available for $4.99/month or $49.99/year.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Since subscriptions are processed through Apple, you'll need to cancel through your Apple ID settings. Open the Settings app on your device, tap your name at the top, then go to Subscriptions and select HomeFitly. You can also go to Settings > Subscription > Manage Subscription within the HomeFitly app. Your Premium access will continue until the end of your current billing period.
Can I use HomeFitly on multiple devices?
Yes. Simply sign in with the same account on any device. Your data syncs across all your devices automatically. If you have a Premium subscription, use the Restore Purchases option in Settings to activate Premium on additional devices.
How do I delete my account and data?
You can delete your account from within the app by navigating to Settings > Account > Delete Account. This permanently removes all your data, including your home profile, tasks, projects, receipts, and household information. This action cannot be undone. For more details, visit our Account Deletion page.
Is my data secure?
Absolutely. HomeFitly takes your privacy and data security seriously. We use industry-standard encryption for data in transit and at rest. Your personal information is never sold to third parties. For full details, please review our Privacy Policy.
